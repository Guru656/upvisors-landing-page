Where we are in our journey:

We are a boutique M&A advisory. We run the buy-side agenda, for US IT companies targeting inorganic entry followed by sustainable growth in Europe.

Let's Unpack This

  1. Buy-side agenda: Our focus revolves around buy-side activities, encompassing tailored introductions, best-fit candidates, skilful bridging of negotiations and facilitation of due diligence.
  2. US IT specialization: We serve US-based IT companies, ensuring that our specialization is fine-tuned to cater to the intricacies of U.S. enterprises seeking European business growth.
  3. Enabling sustained growth: Our commitment doesn't conclude with strategic market entry. We pave the way for robust expansion within the European market, during and beyond the earnout phase.
  4. European market emphasis: Our primary geographical emphasis lies in Europe, where we leverage our specialized knowledge, extensive networks, and dedicated resources to bolster successful market entry and foster growth.
  5. Exclusivity-driven process: We meticulously select M&A candidates that seamlessly align with our clients' strategic visions. Exclusivity is maintained throughout the process, with the lock-in commitment enduring until the due diligence phase reaches completion.
