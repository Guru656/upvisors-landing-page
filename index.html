Who we are and Where we are in our journey:

We work as buy-side advisors for US-based tech companies. We provide bespoke solutions for strategic entry and sustainable growth into the European market.

At present, we are a boutique M&A advisory with a successful track record of assisting acquisitions that have proven to be launching pads for entry and sustainable growth in Europe.

Let's Unpack This

  1. Buy-side agenda: Our focus revolves around buy-side activities, encompassing 1. Tailored finding & introductions to "Best-fit candidates", 2. Skilful bridging of negotiations and 3. Facilitation of due diligence. If you are looking for an exit, then there is not much we can do to help you sell. However, we do encourage sellers to register their interest with us.
  2. US Tech specialization: We serve only US-based "Tech" companies, ensuring that we remain highly focussed and our specialization is fine-tuned to cater to the intricacies of U.S. enterprises seeking European business growth.
  3. Enabling sustained growth: Our commitment doesn't conclude with strategic market entry. We pave the way for robust expansion within the European market, during and beyond the earnout phase.
  4. European market emphasis: Our geographical focus lies in Europe, where we leverage our specialized knowledge, extensive networks, and dedicated resources to bolster successful market entry and foster growth.
  5. Exclusivity-driven process: We meticulously select M&A candidates that sign up for "Exclusivity" with a lock-in commitment enduring until the due diligence phase reaches completion.
